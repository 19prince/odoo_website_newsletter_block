## Decisions Made

- XPath `//div[1]` with `position="before"` — mirrors upstream `website_mass_mailing` snippet options registration pattern exactly
- No `data-target` attribute — defaults `$target` to the matched `.js_subscribe` element, which is where `_getListId()` reads `data-list-id` (no traversal needed)
- No `recaptchaSubscribe` div — upstream already handles `.s_newsletter_list`; adding for our selector risks double-render since we share `.js_subscribe` class
- `data-attribute-name="listId"` camelCase critical — `mailing_list_subscribe._renderCustomXML` queries `we-select[data-attribute-name="listId"]` exactly; wrong case silently breaks list-id write
